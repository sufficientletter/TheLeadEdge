Quick Questions

Is this allowed by the MLS?
100% yes. Back-office data feeds are explicitly authorized by NAR policy (Statement 8.7) and supported by NABOR. Every major brokerage uses them for their CRM and analytics tools. Your data stays internal — it's never displayed publicly.
Will other agents see my data or know I'm using this?
No. This is a private, back-office connection between your MLS account and our system. No one else can see your queries or your lead scores. It's your competitive intelligence, invisible to everyone else.
What if I cancel my MLS membership?
If your membership lapses, the API access stops automatically. There are no long-term contracts — it's just an annual license tied to your MLS membership.
Can I still use the CSV exports too?
Yes. The system supports both. The API just means you don't have to do manual exports anymore. If you ever want to run a special one-off search and export it, that still works.
What's "Trestle"?
Trestle is the data platform made by CoreLogic — the same company that runs Matrix (what you log into every day to search listings). Think of it as the "back door" to Matrix that lets software pull data automatically instead of you clicking around manually.
